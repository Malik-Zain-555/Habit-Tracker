# Privacy Policy for HabStreak

## Our Commitment to Privacy

HabStreak is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining transparency about how your data is handled.

## Data Collection and Storage

- **Local Storage**: All data you enter in the app (habits, streaks, notes) is stored locally on your device by default
- **Cloud Storage**: If you opt into cloud features, your data is securely encrypted and handled through Firebase services
- **No Third-Party Sharing**: We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties
- **Minimal Collection**: We collect no sensitive personal information
